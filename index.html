About us

Welcome to Government Graduate College for Women Shahdara

Established in 1982, Government Graduate College for Women Shahdara is affiliated with the University of the Punjab and offers a wide range of intermediate and undergraduate programs including F.A, F.Sc, I.Com, B.A, B.Sc, B.B.A and B.C.S. We are committed to providing quality education in a supportive, research‑oriented environment where young women can become confident and empowered leaders.

Our Facilities

  • Science & Computer Labs

    State‑of‑the‑art laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, Biology and Computer Science provide hands‑on learning experiences and support our science and technology programmes.

  • Library & Reading Rooms

    A well‑stocked library with course texts, reference books and digital resources, plus quiet reading rooms, supports academic research and independent study.

  • Sports & Societies

    Students enjoy a range of sports—including badminton, volleyball and hockey—and participate in debating, science and cultural societies to build confidence and team‑spirit.

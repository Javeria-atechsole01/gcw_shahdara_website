Enrollment Statistics

Below are the latest figures for student enrollment at Government Graduate College for Women Shahdara Lahore. These charts illustrate the number of students in each class and in various academic programmes. The data is based on the 2023–2024 academic year and is subject to change as new students enroll.

Students in Classes

This bar chart displays the distribution of students across the first and second year of Intermediate studies and each semester of the BS programmes. The college currently has larger cohorts in 1st Year and 2nd Year F.Sc/F.A classes, with smaller but growing numbers in the senior semesters of our four‑year programmes.

Students in Programmes

The chart on the right illustrates enrollment in specific programmes such as F.A, HECO (Home Economics), various Associate Degree programmes, and BS specialisations including English Linguistics, Urdu and Commerce. These figures show the popularity of our programmes and guide future planning.

If you require more detailed information or have questions about enrolment statistics, please contact our admissions office. We regularly update these figures to reflect the current status of our student body.

Government Graduate College for Women Shahdara Lahore is committed to empowering women through quality education and research‑oriented learning. We offer a range of programmes from intermediate to undergraduate level and foster a supportive environment for academic excellence.

Admissions

Admissions Criteria

The academic session starts in September and concludes in June. Admission is granted strictly on merit in accordance with the schedule announced by the college. Applicants may obtain a prospectus from the college after paying the required fee. Merit lists of provisionally selected candidates are displayed on the college notice board; selected candidates must pay their admission dues by the notified dates or their admission shall be cancelled.

Intermediate Programmes

  • The student must have passed the Secondary School Certificate (Matriculation) or O‑Level examination in the 1st Annual.
  • The maximum age limit is 18 years (extendable to 20 years for fresh candidates of the 1st Annual).

Note: After taking admission in this college, students registered with any other institution must cancel their previous registration. Submission of fake documents may result in cancellation of admission at any stage.

Government Graduate College for Women Shahdara is dedicated to empowering women through quality education, research and holistic development. We strive to cultivate excellence and inspire lifelong learning in a supportive environment.

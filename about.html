About us

Welcome to Government Graduate College for Women Shahdara

Government Graduate College for Women Shahdara Lahore was established in 1982 and is recognised by the University of the Punjab. The college offers a wide range of intermediate and undergraduate programmes—from F.Sc and F.A to B.A, B.Sc, B.B.A and B.C.S—and provides a research‑oriented environment where students are encouraged to excel academically and personally. Our dedicated faculty, modern facilities and supportive campus help to empower women through education.

01

Why Choose Us

Affiliated with the University of the Punjab, our college offers state‑of‑the‑art laboratories, a well‑stocked library and dedicated faculty. We emphasise research, discipline and character‑building in a safe and supportive environment tailored specifically for women.

02

Our Mission

To provide quality education in a supportive environment, developing confident, disciplined and responsible individuals, and empowering them through modern facilities and vibrant co‑curricular activities.

03

Our Vision

To empower women by nurturing intellectual growth, character development and leadership qualities so they can contribute positively to their communities and the nation.
